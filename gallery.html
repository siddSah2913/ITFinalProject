About Mithila Art in Nepal: Many of the paintings in this gallery reflect the living tradition practised across Nepal's Madhesh Province - from the Janakpur Women's Development Centre to village homes in Saptari, Siraha, Mahottari, and beyond. The fish, lotus, and deity motifs seen here are painted on home walls across the region to mark weddings, festivals, and auspicious occasions.
